What is Snowflake Cortex?

Snowflake Cortex is an intelligent, fully managed service that provides access to large language models (LLMs) and AI models. It enables organizations to quickly and easily analyze data and build AI applications, all within Snowflake. This service aims to democratize AI, making it accessible to all business users to drive business value.

Key Features of Snowflake Cortex

  • LLM Functions: Access state-of-the-art large language models for tasks like sentiment analysis, translation, and summarization directly from SQL.
  • ML-Based Functions: Use machine learning models for forecasting, anomaly detection, and classification without needing data science expertise.
  • Studio: A user-friendly interface for building and deploying AI applications using a no-code/low-code approach.
  • Secure and Governed: All processing happens within your Snowflake account, ensuring your data remains secure and governed by your existing policies.
