A Note on Compute Costs

While many learning resources are free, remember that using Snowflake Cortex features in your own account incurs compute costs, typically based on token usage. Snowflake trial accounts have limitations, so for full access, you may need to convert to a paid account. Managing costs by choosing appropriate models and monitoring usage is a key part of practical Cortex expertise.
